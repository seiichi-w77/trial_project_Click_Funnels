This article explains how to manage course lessons, which are designed to empower you with essential skills for optimizing the learning experience of your course. With detailed references on accessing, adding, duplicating, and deleting lessons, you can easily navigate your course content and customize the learning journey of your course.

Requirements

An active ClickFunnels 2.0 account

An existing Course

An existing Module

The Courses App (Adding Apps to Your ClickFunnels Workspace)

Accessing Course Lessons

Click Courses from the APPS navigation menu.

NOTE

Please visit our article Adding Apps to Your ClickFunnels Workspace for detailed information on how to add apps to your navigation menu

Click the Title of the course.

NOTE

The course lessons are displayed once you enter the course dashboard. Optionally, click the “Expand All” button to the left of the “Add Content” button to expand the modules and display lessons.

Adding a New Lesson

Click the Add Content button at the right-hand side of the page, then select Add new lesson from the drop-down menu, or click the Add Content link to the right of each module, then select Add Lesson from the drop-down menu to add a new lesson.


Optionally, click the Parent Module drop-down to verify the correct Module where the lesson will be added is correct, then fill out the Title field.

Click Create lesson to save your changes.

Editing the Lesson Settings and Content

Click the lesson's Title to open the editor or the three-dot menu, then select Edit Lesson from the drop-down menu.


You will now find yourself in the Lesson Editor where you can edit the Lesson Settings and Lesson Content. Please visit our article Lesson Settings for more information about the options in the lesson settings.

NOTE

The Lesson Settings are located on the left-hand side of the lesson editor where you can customize the Title, Description, Multimedia, and Path of the lesson.

The Lesson Editor is located at the center, where you can add the Sections, Rows, and Elements for the lesson.

Add a Lesson from inside the lesson editor by clicking the Add Lesson button at the top of the modal.

All new lessons created from inside the lesson editor are placed last in the lesson’s order. Arrange the lesson’s order by left-clicking, then holding and dragging the lesson from the course dashboard.

Quickly jump and edit lessons by clicking the “previous” or “next” arrowa to the left of the Add a Lesson button.

Duplicating a Lesson

Click the three-dot icon on the right of the lesson you want to duplicate, then select Duplicate Lesson from the drop-down menu.

Click the Proceed button to duplicate the lesson or the Cancel button to cancel any changes.

A banner will appear at the top-right corner of the page explaining that the duplicated lesson will appear once the duplicate is completed.

Deleting a Course Lesson

Click the three-dot menu icon on the right of the lesson you want to delete, then select Delete Lesson from the drop-down menu. 

Click the Proceed button to delete the lesson. Otherwise, click the Cancel button to cancel any changes.

WARNING

Deleting an lesson is permanent and cannot be undone.

The app displays a confirmation banner stating that the lesson has been deleted.
