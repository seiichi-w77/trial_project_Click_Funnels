Migrating funnels from ClickFunnels Classic can be time-consuming if you need to manually rebuild your existing funnel pages. BarnumPT offers a powerful solution by providing a browser extension that lets you quickly copy and migrate pages directly into ClickFunnels (formerly ClickFunnels 2.0). In this article, you'll learn how to use the BarnumPT extension to streamline your page migration process with just a few clicks.


Requirements

An active ClickFunnels account

Existing funnel pages in ClickFunnels Classic

What is BarnumPT

BarnumPT is a suite of tools created by Russell Brunson and Wynter Jones to support marketers and funnel builders in optimizing their workflow across ClickFunnels platforms. While it offers several features, such as wireframe tools, design utilities, and productivity enhancements, this article focuses specifically on using the BarnumPT browser extension to migrate funnel pages from ClickFunnels Classic into ClickFunnels. The extension makes it easy to copy and import your existing pages directly into the ClickFunnels Page Editor, ensuring a smooth and consistent transition.

Step 1: Install the BarnumPT Browser Extension

Visit the BarnumPT website and create a free account.

Login to your BarnumPT account.

Navigate to the Install tab.

Download and install the BarnumPT Browser Extension on Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Microsoft Edge.


Once installed, the BarnumPT extension (Hat icon) will appear in your browser toolbar.

Learn more about the installation process at BarnumPT help center.

Step 2: Copy a Funnel Page from ClickFunnels Classic

Log in to your ClickFunnels Classic account.

Open the Funnel that contains the page you want to migrate.

Click Edit Page to enter the page editor.

Once your edits are complete, click Preview (üëÅÔ∏è) to view the page.


On the previewed page, click the BarnumPT Extension (Hat üé© Icon) in your browser toolbar.

Click Save Page as Copy. BarnumPT will now save your current page.


In the open prompt, allow permission to copy the page and click Copy Page to start the transfer.

BarnumPT will replicate the page and present recommendations. Please review the recommendations to determine if any elements are unsupported for transfer. If any elements can not be transferred, you must manually add them to the page editor after completing the migrations.

Note:

While BarnumPT significantly streamlines the migration process and saves time, ClickFunnels cannot guarantee full compatibility of content, layout structure, colors, fonts, or mobile optimization. We recommend reviewing each imported page carefully and making necessary adjustments in the ClickFunnels Editor. For technical support or troubleshooting, please contact the BarnumPT support team.

Step 3: Migrate the Page into ClickFunnels (Formerly CF 2.0)

Log in to your ClickFunnels account.

Go to the location where you want to migrate the page (e.g., Funnel, Site, Landing Pages).

Enter the Page Editor of the selected page.

Click on the BarnumPT Assistant Avatar at the top-left of the editor.


Choose Copied Pages from the popup menu.


Select the copied page you want to migrate and click Import.

Wait a moment while BarnumPT migrates the page contents into the editor.

After migration, ensure that everything looks and works as expected and make any necessary edits.

Note:

The contents imported onto the page will match the design from the ClickFunnels Classic. Imported contents (Sections, Rows, and Elements) does not follow the style guide linked to the page settings.

BarnumPT may not consistently import special elements (e.g., Social Share, Survey, etc.). Please review the BarnumPT recommendations after copying the page. You can add them manually after the migration.

Please refer to the BarnumPT help center for any further information and assistance.
