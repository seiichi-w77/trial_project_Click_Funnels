The command "Set_Marketing_KPIs" requires defining Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) across the marketing funnel for the "Miyabi AGI OS & AI Dreams Factory" project and establishing a tracking plan.

Based on the project context, the primary marketing objectives are to:
*   Elevate the Japanese generative AI video industry.
*   Foster robust Japan-China creator collaboration.
*   Increase the adoption of BytePlus AI models and services.
*   Expand the user base and contractual engagements for Miyabi AGI OS.
*   Catalyze the development and commercialization of new AI content and products through the 'AI Dreams Factory' initiative.
*   Cultivate a sustainable ecosystem where creators can thrive financially through AI-driven endeavors.
*   Promote unique Japanese AI methodologies, such as Shunsuke's World Model Logic (SWML), globally.

Below are the defined KPIs for each stage of the marketing funnel and a comprehensive tracking plan.

---

## 1. Marketing Funnel & Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### A. Awareness (認知)
**Goal:** Increase visibility, brand recognition, and general understanding of Miyabi AGI OS, the AI Dreams Factory, and the broader Japan-China AI collaboration initiative.

*   **Website/Platform Traffic:**
    *   Unique Visitors (UV) to Miyabi AGI OS landing pages, AI Dreams Factory website, and event registration portals.
    *   Traffic Sources (e.g., organic search, social media, direct, referrals from WAY to AGI, Shift AI).
    *   New vs. Returning Visitors.
*   **Social Media Reach & Impressions:**
    *   Total reach and impressions across key platforms (e.g., X/Twitter, LinkedIn, WeChat, YouTube).
    *   Brand mentions, shares, and follower growth on official channels.
*   **Media Coverage & PR:**
    *   Number of articles, blog posts, or news features in AI/tech publications.
    *   Estimated Media Value (EMV).
*   **Event Attendance:**
    *   Number of registrations and actual attendees for online and offline events (e.g., December ByteDance HQ event, workshops, hackathons).

### B. Consideration (検討)
**Goal:** Engage potential users and partners, encourage deeper exploration of value propositions, and foster active evaluation of Miyabi, BytePlus, and participation opportunities within the AI Dreams Factory.

*   **Engagement Rate:**
    *   Average time spent on Miyabi AGI OS documentation, API guides, and AI Dreams Factory information pages.
    *   Bounce Rate on key informational pages.
    *   Video views and completion rates for Miyabi demos, hackathon highlights, and technical tutorials.
    *   Interaction rates (likes, comments, shares) on technical or in-depth content.
*   **Content Downloads/Access:**
    *   Downloads of whitepapers, technical specifications, and API documentation.
    *   Active users/access frequency for the Japanese AI knowledge base on Lark (translated/co-created from Feishu).
*   **Community Interaction:**
    *   Number of active members and new sign-ups in community groups (e.g., Lark, WeChat, Discord).
    *   Number of technical questions posted and resolved within community forums.
    *   Participation rate in interactive sessions (e.g., Q&A during webinars, live coding events).
*   **Hackathon/Workshop Completion:**
    *   Number of participants successfully completing hackathon challenges or workshops.
    *   Qualitative assessment of project submissions (e.g., innovative use of BytePlus APIs, Miyabi features).
*   **Demo/Trial Requests:**
    *   Number of requests for Miyabi AGI OS demonstrations or BytePlus API trial accounts.

### C. Conversion (コンバージョン)
**Goal:** Drive tangible adoption, active participation, and generate measurable business outcomes, including new contracts, active usage, and project initiations.

*   **Miyabi AGI OS Adoption:**
    *   Number of new Miyabi AGI OS user sign-ups and contractual agreements.
    *   Number of active development projects managed through Miyabi.
    *   Code generation volume (e.g., total lines of code generated by Miyabi for clients per reporting period).
    *   Demonstrated improvement in development metrics (e.g., reduction in cycle time, increase in sprint velocity as reported by users).
*   **BytePlus API Usage:**
    *   Number of new BytePlus API keys issued and consistently active.
    *   Volume and frequency of API calls to Seedream, Vision API, and other BytePlus services.
    *   Successful integration rate of BytePlus APIs into external projects.
*   **AI Dreams Factory Project Incubation:**
    *   Number of new AI product ideas successfully initiated and prototyped within the factory framework.
    *   Number of projects progressing from prototype to commercialization phase.
    *   Revenue generated by commercially launched products from the factory.
*   **Partnership & Collaboration Outcomes:**
    *   Number of new strategic collaborations established (e.g., with corporations, educational institutions, other communities).
    *   Successful co-development projects between Japanese and Chinese creators/teams.
*   **Monetization Success:**
    *   Total revenue generated by creators and projects leveraging the AI Dreams Factory or Miyabi.
    *   Number of creators reporting sustainable income streams derived from AI-driven content or products.
    *   Sales performance of commercialized AI tools or content.

## 2. Tracking Plan

### A. Data Collection Tools
*   **Web Analytics Platforms:** Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, or similar for website traffic, user behavior, and content engagement.
*   **Social Media Analytics:** Native platforms (e.g., X Analytics, LinkedIn Analytics, WeChat insights, YouTube Analytics) for reach, impressions, engagement, and follower growth.
*   **CRM & Sales Systems:** Salesforce, HubSpot, or custom systems to track Miyabi contracts, BytePlus API subscriptions, lead management, and partner agreements.
*   **GitHub Metrics:** GitHub API and custom scripts to monitor Miyabi-driven project activity, code generation, and issue/PR resolution.
*   **BytePlus Developer Console:** Direct access to BytePlus API usage statistics (call volume, active keys, error rates).
*   **Event Management Software:** Eventbrite, Zoom Events, or similar for registration, attendance, and post-event feedback.
*   **Community Platforms:** Lark, WeChat, Discord analytics or manual monitoring for active member count, discussion volume, and content sharing.
*   **Surveys & Feedback Mechanisms:** Online survey tools (e.g., Google Forms, SurveyMonkey) to collect qualitative data on user satisfaction, feature requests, and collaboration interest.

### B. Reporting Frequency
*   **Weekly Dashboards:** Concise overview of top-level Awareness and Consideration KPIs (e.g., website traffic, social media reach, event registrations) to identify immediate trends or issues.
*   **Monthly Performance Reports:** Detailed analysis of all KPIs, including performance against targets, growth trends, source attribution, and deep dives into active user/project metrics.
*   **Quarterly Strategic Reviews:** Comprehensive assessment of long-term trends, monetization progress, strategic adjustments based on ecosystem development, and ROI analysis of key initiatives. This will include evaluating progress towards the 2-3 month foundational goals and the 12+ month global expansion targets.

### C. Target Setting
*   **Establish Baselines:** Collect current data for existing Miyabi engagements, community activity, and BytePlus API usage to set realistic starting points.
*   **SMART Targets:** Define Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound targets for each KPI. Examples:
    *   "Increase unique visitors to Miyabi AGI OS landing pages by 30% month-over-month for the next 3 months."
    *   "Secure 5 new Miyabi AGI OS contracts by Q1 2025."
    *   "Achieve a 15% increase in BytePlus Seedream API call volume by Q2 2025."
    *   "Launch 3 new AI Dreams Factory incubated products to market by Q3 2025."
*   **Phased Targets:** Align KPI targets with the project's strategic phases (Foundation, Market Activation, Ecosystem Industrialization, Global Expansion).

### D. Ownership & Review Process
*   **Clear Ownership:** Assign specific team members or departments (e.g., Marketing, Business Development, Product Management, Community Management) responsibility for tracking, analyzing, and reporting on each KPI.
*   **Regular Review Meetings:**
    *   **Weekly Stand-ups:** Quick reviews of critical KPIs to ensure alignment and address immediate operational challenges.
    *   **Monthly KPI Deep Dives:** Comprehensive discussions on performance, trend analysis, identification of bottlenecks, and strategy refinement.
    *   **Quarterly Strategic Planning Sessions:** High-level review with leadership to evaluate overall project health, adjust long-term vision, and allocate resources effectively.
